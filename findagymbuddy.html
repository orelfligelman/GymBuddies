FAQ

GymBuddies wants to make the gym a social place. We think everytime you go to lift or to run or to space out, you should have the option to go do it with a friend. Working out with someone usually makes the workout more fun and it helps motivate you to keep coming back to the gym.

Isn't it weird to workout with a stranger?

At gymbuddies, our philosophy is everyone is your friend, you just haven't become friends with them yet. We don't think the lumberjack deadlifting 500 pounds and excreting 4.7 ounces of saliva while doing it has nothing in common with you. We help bridge that gap in a safe and friendly manner.

Why don't I just hit up my friends?

Of course you can. It's tough because you're friends have a time schedule that might not be yours, fitness goals that you may not share, a refusal to shower that just doesn't jive with you, or they are a convicted felon you can't be seen with.
